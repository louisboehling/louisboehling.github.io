About Me

I am a graphic designer from Richmond, Virginia who is currently exploring the quickly expanding world of web development. I have been working as a graphic designer for 4 years in Richmond and in Hoi An, Vietnam. Outside of work I enjoy hiking, going to the gym, cooking healthy food, reading, and contributing to independent travel publications.

I wanted to get into coding because I belive that a graphic designer with well-rounded full-stack coding abilities is rare. In an agency setting I hope I will be able to oscillate between roles as a designer and as a front-end and back-end developer.
